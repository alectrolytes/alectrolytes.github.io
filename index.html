Yo, I'm Alec.

I am a curious extrovert. Although I currently study pharmacy at the Philadelphia College of Pharmacy, I have a fascination for knowledge. My love for critical thinking and problem solving is matched only by my habit of creating them. I enjoy multiplayer videogames, computer programming, and ultimate frisbee. I dislike surrendering, recursion, and sweets.

Team Nexus is an organization where awesome ideas converge. I am the only permanent member. The other members are the inspiring people who have helped or collaborated with me. I learn from others and try to emulate them with my own style. Without the wonderful help and positive influence of the people around me, I would not get where I am today. These lessons are treasured and referenced to achieve success.

You can email me at: alec@teamnex.us
