Yo, I'm Alec.

I am a curious extrovert who currently studies pharmacy at the Philadelphia College of Pharmacy. I have a fascination for knowledge. My love for solving problems is matched only by my habit of creating them. Although ambitious, my life goals are to do everything, become awesome, and inspire others. I enjoy multiplayer videogames, computer programming, and ultimate frisbee. I dislike surrendering, recursion, and sweets.

My plan for the immediate future is to explore the confluence of medicine and technology. I have worked in retail and hospital settings, but I think I belong in non-traditional settings. I am interested in informatics pharmacy which focuses on different systems in the healthcare workflow.

Team Nexus is a representation of the convergence of experiences with everyone I have met. Succeeding at life is a team effort. These heroes teach and inspire me to emulate them with my own style.
