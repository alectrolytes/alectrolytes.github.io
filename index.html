Yo, I'm Alec.

I am a curious extrovert. Although I currently study pharmacy at the Philadelphia College of Pharmacy, I have a fascination for knowledge. My love for critical thinking and problem solving is matched only by my habit of creating them. Although ambitious, my life goals are to do everything, become awesome, and inspire others. My I enjoy multiplayer videogames, computer programming, and ultimate frisbee. I dislike surrendering, recursion, and sweets.

My plan for the immediate future is to explore the various fields of pharmacy while completing my education and earning my PharmD. I currently work in a retail pharmacy, but am more interested in clinically-challenging setting. Due to my passion of technology, I am interested in informatics pharmacy which focuses on different technology systems in the healthcare workflow.

Team Nexus is an organization where awesome ideas converge. I am the only permanent member. The other members are the inspiring people who have helped or collaborated with me. I learn from others and try to emulate them with my own style. Without the wonderful help and positive influence of the people around me, I would not get where I am today. These lessons are treasured and referenced to achieve success.

You can email me at: alec@teamnex.us
